## About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Economics at LSE, working both empirically and experimentally, and with a little bit of theory. I broadly focus on the organisation of work, economics and psychology with an interest in mental wellbeing, and frequently find myself working with generative AI or studying its implications. I am also an affiliate at the [Altruistic Capital Lab](https://altruisticcapitallab.com/) and a Ph.D. affiliate at the [LSE Data Science Institute](https://www.lse.ac.uk/dsi/about).
